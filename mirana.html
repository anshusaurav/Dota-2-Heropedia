Starstorm

Calls down a wave of meteors to damage nearby enemy units. The closest enemy unit to Mirana in a 425 radius will be struck a second time for 75% of the damage.

Sacred Arrow

Fires a long-range arrow with deadly precision, which stuns and damages the first enemy unit it strikes. The stun duration ranges from 0.01 to 3.5 seconds, with bonus damage up to 180 added, based on the distance the arrow travels to its target. Instantly kills the first non-ancient creep it hits.

Leap

Mirana leaps forward into battle, empowering herself with a ferocious roar that grants bonus attack and movement speed. Speed bonus lasts 2.5 seconds.

Moonlight Shadow

Turns Mirana and all allied heroes invisible and grants bonus movment speed. If a hero is revealed, invisibility will restore after the fade delay as long as Moonlight Shadow's duration has not expired.
